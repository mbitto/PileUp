Drag a circle over a larger circle to pile it up. The more similar is the size the more points you get

Tap over a pile to pop out the first circle. Doing this you'll loose the double of the points you got when you piled it up

On every move you make a new circle will appear on stage. The same will happen if you don't do nothing for some time

Every level defines the number of circles to complete a pile as well as the number of piles to complete the game
